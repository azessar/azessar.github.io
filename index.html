Reztaurant

Reztaurant is an OpenTable.com-inspired web application that allows users to interact with their favorite TV-show restaurants by making mock reservations and writing reviews. This project gave me great experience working with the Ruby/Rails, Javascript, and React/Redux fullstack. Log in as demo-user Gordon Ramsay and leave a review for The Krusty Krab!

EtymoloGenius

EtymoloGenius goes out to all you lingusitics nerds out there. Simply enter a word and watch EtymoloGenius track that word's etymological path from its origin country and language to English (represented by the UK). This project allowed me to demonstrate my vanilla JavaScript skills to make something visually appealing. I also enjoyed working with language; the most challenging yet fun part was figuring out the best way to parse through the Oxford Dictionary's etymology texts to put the relevant countries in the correct order.

Am I The Moral One?

Are you the moral one? Inspired by the subreddit AITA, AITMO seeks to answer that question by having users answer controversial questions and compare their answers to the general population. Users can also upload their own quizzes and see how others answer their questions.
